Who We Are

Fear of Bot is a rookie FTC robotics team based in the Washington DC area. We're a passionate group of student engineers, programmers, and problem-solvers competing at the highest level of FIRST Tech Challenge.

FIRST Tech Challenge challenges teams to design, build, program, and operate robots to complete game tasks against competing alliances. Every season brings a new challenge, and we bring everything we have.

In our debut season we competed at two regional qualifiers, earned the Design Award at Alexandria VA Qualifier I, and were selected for playoff alliances, all in Year 1.
