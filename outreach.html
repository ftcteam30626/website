Our Outreach Mission

Fear of Bot is committed to building awareness of FIRST Tech Challenge and inspiring students of all ages to pursue robotics and STEM education. We engage directly with our community through events, hands-on demonstrations, and mentorship opportunities.

Outreach Events

We participated in several community events throughout the 2025-26 season, bringing hands-on robotics experiences to families and students of all ages.

What We Do

Our Activities

Hands-On Robotics Experiences

We provide interactive demonstrations where community members can see our robot in action, learn about subsystems, and understand the engineering behind FTC robotics.

Interactive Robot Operation

We encourage visitors to take control of our robot, experiencing firsthand the complexity and excitement of operating a competition-ready FTC robot.

FIRST Program Awareness

We share information about the FIRST program, FTC, and career opportunities in STEM with families and students interested in robotics and engineering.

Family Engagement

We connect with parents and students who show interest in FIRST, providing guidance about joining FTC teams or starting FLL teams.

Community Network Building

Throughout our outreach efforts, we have collected contact information from approximately 125 community members interested in FIRST, creating a network for future engagement.

Our Community Partners
